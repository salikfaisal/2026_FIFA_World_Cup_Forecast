Most Likely Bracket

This visual bracket shows the most common matchup in each knockout slot across 10,000 simulations. The winner listed is the team that most often won that specific matchup when it occurred.

This bracket should not be read as one single simulated tournament path. Each matchup slot is based on the most common outcome for that specific slot across all simulations. Because of that, winners from earlier rounds may not always line up perfectly with the most likely matchups shown in later rounds.
